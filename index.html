Selected Work

Products We've Shipped

A few of the solutions we've built — each one started with a real market gap.

TrackFlow

Sector: Logistics — UAE

Last-mile Logistics SaaS Platform

Key Success Point:

Delivery businesses in the UAE were running dispatch on WhatsApp groups and spreadsheets. TrackFlow gave them a real-time driver tracking dashboard, automated route assignment, and proof-of-delivery — cutting manual ops by 80% in the first month.

80% reduction in manual dispatch operations

Hadir

Sector: EdTech — Saudi Arabia

Education Mobile Application

Key Success Point:

Schools in Saudi Arabia were tracking attendance with paper registers. Hadir replaced that with a mobile app for teachers, automated SMS alerts to parents, and a real-time dashboard for administrators — adopted by 12 schools in the first quarter.

12 schools onboarded in the first quarter
